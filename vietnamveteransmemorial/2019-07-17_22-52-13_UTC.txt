♤September 24, 1967♤
Mobile Riverine Force (MRF) with the barracks ship USS Colleton APB 36 at anchor in the Mekong Delta. Colleton provided berthing accommodations for all the troops of the MRF. She was also equipped with a floating pier for all smaller boats and a helipad to accommodate air traffic.
