The Three Soldiers (also known as The Three Servicemen) on the National Mall in Washington, DC. This bronze #statue by Frederick Hart was unveiled on Veterans Day, November 11, 1984. It is part of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial and the first representation of an African American on the National Mall.
