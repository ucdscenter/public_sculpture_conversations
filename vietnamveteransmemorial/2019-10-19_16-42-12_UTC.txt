People from near and far came to pay their respects to Vietnam veterans when The Moving Wall was in Mint Hill from Oct. 10 to 14 🇺🇸
***
The Moving Wall is a mobile half-size replica of the memorial in Washington, D.C., to the 2.7 million men and women who served in Vietnam, etched with the 58,276 names of those who made the ultimate sacrifice ❤️ It has been touring the country for more than 30 years.
