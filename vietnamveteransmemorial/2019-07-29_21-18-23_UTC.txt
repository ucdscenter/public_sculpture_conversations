We are proud and honored to announce that Hirschbach was selected as a 2019 Haul the Wall carrier for the mobile replica of the Vietnam Veterans Memorial - The Wall that Heals. 
Hirschbach was selected to Haul the Wall from Stillwater, MN to Ossipee, NH, with multiple drivers taking different legs of the journey. Richard Flegal and his military wrapped truck, have the first leg and arrived at our corporate office in Dubuque today! #weloveourveterans #vietnamveteransmemorial
