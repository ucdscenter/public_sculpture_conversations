♤June 4, 1966♤
A Marine with his M-14 across his lap takes a drink out of his canteen, while sitting next to a sign which reads “Oakland, Calif. 11,000 mi.” This Marine was part of the 1st Battalion, 4th Regiment, of the 3rd Marine Division, his division was positioned near Hue, farther north than any other U.S. combat unit in Vietnam at the time.
