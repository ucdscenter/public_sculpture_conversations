Some of the most picturesque clouds occur close to sunrise and sunset when they can appear in brilliant yellows, oranges reds, pinks and purples. The colors result from a combination of Rayleigh and Mie scattering.

So I tried painting this beautiful brilliant pink and purple cloud of sunset where you can see appearance of the moon from the back. .
